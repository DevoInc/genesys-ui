<H1>Label</H1>

<H2>Overview</H2>

Component used to provide textual labels for other components, helping to identify their purpose or provide additional context to users.
By associating a label with a specific form control, users can more easily understand what type of data should be entered and why.
The Label component can be used in conjunction with a variety of other components, such as input fields, checkboxes, or radio buttons, to create a complete form element.
