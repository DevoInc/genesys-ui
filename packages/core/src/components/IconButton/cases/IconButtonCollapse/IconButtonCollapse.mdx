<H1>IconButtonCollapse</H1>

<H2>Overview</H2>

The IconButtonCollapse is a special type of IconButton
used to toggle the visibility
of a block of content by collapsing or uncollapsing it.
It is commonly used in accordions or collapsible sections where you
want to show or hide a section of content based on user interaction.
The IconButtonCollapse component displays an arrow facing up or down
to indicate the state of the content block.
