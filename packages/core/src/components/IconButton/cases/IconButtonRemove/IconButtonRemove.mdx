<H1>IconButtonRemove</H1>

<H2>Overview</H2>

The IconButtonRemove is a special type of IconButton used to
remove a block of content that is included in the regular flow of the DOM, such as banners, filter chips, and other similar components.
It provides an easy way for users to remove the content with a single click.
The IconButtonRemove is rendered as an icon button with cross icon,
and it typically appears next to or within the content it is associated with.
